English: "How much does this cost?"

Russian:

Сколько это стоит? (Skol'ko eto stoit?)

Сколько = "How much."

Это = "this."

Стоит = "costs" (from стоить, "to cost").


Spanish:

¿Cuánto cuesta esto?

¿Cuánto cuesta…? = "How much does … cost?"

Esto = "this" (neutral pronoun).


Chinese:

这个多少钱？ (Zhège duōshǎo qián?)

这个 = "this."

多少钱？ = "how much money?" (word order is different from English).


French:

Combien ça coûte ?

Combien = "How much."

Ça coûte = "this costs" (from coûter, "to cost").


Arabic:

بكم هذا؟ (Bikam hādhā?)

بكم = "how much (price)."

هذا = "this" (masculine).


Korean:

이거 얼마예요? (Igeo eolmayeyo?)

이거 = "this" (casual).

얼마예요? = "how much is it?"


Japanese:

これはいくらですか？ (Kore wa ikura desu ka?)

これは = "this (thing)."

いくらですか？ = "how much is it?"


German:

Wie viel kostet das?

Wie viel = "how much."

Kostet = "costs" (from kosten, "to cost").

Das = "this" (neutral).
