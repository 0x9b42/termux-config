Here’s your next phrase:

English: "Where is the bathroom?"
Example answer: "The bathroom is over there."

Russian:

Где находится туалет? (Gde nakhoditsya tualet?)

Где = "Where."

Находится = "is located" (formal verb).

Туалет = "toilet/bathroom."


Example answer:

Туалет там. (Tualet tam.) – "The bathroom is over there."

Spanish:

¿Dónde está el baño?

¿Dónde está…? = "Where is…?"

El baño = "the bathroom."


Example answer:

El baño está allí. – "The bathroom is over there."
