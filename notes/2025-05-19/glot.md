# Day 11 — Talking About the Past (Simple Past / Completed Action)

### Chinese (Simplified)

- Sentence: 我昨天去了学校。
- Pinyin: wǒ zuótiān qù le xuéxiào
- Translation: I went to school yesterday.


### Russian

- Sentence: Вчера я ходил в школу. (m) / ходила (f)
- IPA: [ˈfxʲɪra ja xɐˈdʲil f ʂˈkoɫʊ]
- Translation: I went to school yesterday.


### Spanish

- Sentence: Ayer fui a la escuela.
- IPA: [aˈʝeɾ ˈfwi a la esˈkwela]
- Translation: I went to school yesterday.


### French

- Sentence: Hier, je suis allé à l’école. (m) / allée (f)
- IPA: [jɛʁ ʒə sɥi‿z‿aˈle a lekɔl]
- Translation: I went to school yesterday.


### Arabic (MSA)

- Sentence: ذهبتُ إلى المدرسة أمس.
- IPA: [ðaˈhabtʊ ʔilaː lˈmadrasa ʔams]
- Translation: I went to school yesterday.


### Japanese

- Sentence: 昨日学校へ行きました。
- Romaji: kinō gakkō e ikimashita
- Translation: I went to school yesterday.

Mini-note (all languages): Each uses its regular past-tense or perfective form; adverb for “yesterday” appears early.
