# Day 15 — Talking About Abilities (“Can / Be Able To”)

### Chinese (Simplified)

- Sentence: 我会说英语。
- Pinyin: wǒ huì shuō yīngyǔ  
- Grammar: **会** means “can / know how to” (learned skill)
- Translation: I can speak English.

---

### Russian

- Sentence: Я умею говорить по-английски.  
- IPA: [ja ʊˈmʲe.jʊ ɡəvɐˈrʲitʲ pɐ ɐnˈɡlʲijskʲɪ]  
- Grammar: **Умею** is used for learned skills; **говорить** = “to speak”
- Translation: I can speak English.

---

### Spanish

- Sentence: Sé hablar inglés.  
- IPA: [se aˈβlaɾ iŋˈɡles]  
- Grammar: **Saber + infinitive** = “to know how to (do something)”
- Translation: I can speak English.

---

### French

- Sentence: Je sais parler anglais.  
- IPA: [ʒə sɛ paʁle ɑ̃ɡlɛ]  
- Grammar: **Savoir + infinitive** = “to know how to (do something)”
- Translation: I can speak English.

---

### Arabic (MSA)

- Sentence: أستطيع التحدث بالإنجليزية.  
- IPA: [ʔas.taˈtˤiːʕ at.taˈħad.duθ bil.ʔin.d͡ʒi.liː.zij.ja]  
- Grammar: **أستطيع + verb** = “I can”; **التحدث** = verbal noun “speaking”
- Translation: I can speak English.

---

### Japanese

- Sentence: 私は英語を話せます。  
- Romaji: watashi wa eigo o hanasemasu  
- Grammar: Potential form of **話す** (hanasu): **話せます** = “can speak”
- Translation: I can speak English.
