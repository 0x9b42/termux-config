English: "Do you speak English?"
Example answer: "Yes, I speak English." / "No, I don't speak English."

---

Russian: Вы говорите по-английски? (Vy govorite po-angliyski?)

Вы = "You" (formal or plural).

Говорите = "speak" (formal "you" form).

По-английски = "in English."

Example answer:

Да, я говорю по-английски. (Da, ya govoryu po-angliyski.) – "Yes, I speak English."

Нет, я не говорю по-английски. (Net, ya ne govoryu po-angliyski.) – "No, I don't speak English."
